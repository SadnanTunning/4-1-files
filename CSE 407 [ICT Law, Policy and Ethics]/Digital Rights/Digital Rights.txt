Digital rights are human rights in the digital space. Understanding human rights will help you to understand digital rights better. 

This booklets are friendly guides for you to learn about this.

If you want to read only one document, then read the Alliance_for_Universal_Digital_Right's nine principles (most importantly princpiles 1, 2, 3, 4 & 8)
